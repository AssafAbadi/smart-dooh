# 📱 iPhone Connection Guide - Connect to Your App

## 🎉 **EXPO IS RUNNING IN TUNNEL MODE!**

Tunnel mode means you DON'T need to be on the same WiFi - it works anywhere!

---

## 📱 **HOW TO CONNECT YOUR IPHONE (3 Ways)**

### **Method 1: Camera App** (EASIEST for iPhone!)

1. **Open your iPhone Camera app** (the default camera)
2. **Look at your computer terminal** - find the QR code
3. **Point camera at QR code** on computer screen
4. A **yellow banner** appears: "Open in Expo Go"
5. **Tap the banner**
6. Expo Go opens and loads your app!

---

### **Method 2: Expo Go URL** (If Camera doesn't work)

**In Expo Go app on iPhone:**

1. **Open Expo Go** app
2. **Look for one of these**:
   - Text field at top that says "Enter URL"
   - "Enter URL manually" link at bottom
   - Tab called "Projects" → then look for URL field
3. **Type the tunnel URL** (from terminal, looks like: `exp://abc-123.tunnel.exp.io`)
4. **Tap Go** or **Connect**

**Can't find URL field?** Try:
- Swipe down on the home screen to reveal search/URL bar
- Look for a **"+"** button to add project
- Check the "Projects" tab at bottom

---

### **Method 3: Expo Go QR Scanner** 

1. **Open Expo Go** app
2. Tap **"Scan QR Code"** button (camera icon)
3. **Point at QR code** on computer screen
4. App loads automatically!
